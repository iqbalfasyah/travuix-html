Tentang Wisata

Nusa Penida is an island southeast of Indonesia's island Bali and a district of Klungkung Egency that includes the neighbouring small island of Nusa Lembongan. The Badung Strait separates the island and Bali. The interior of Nusa Penida is hilly with a maximum altitude of 524 meters. It is drier than the nearby island of Bali.
